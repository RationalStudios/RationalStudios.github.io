Our Projects

Annihilation44

Main!

Annihilation44 is an upcoming WW2 game crafted within the powerful Unreal Engine, drawing artistic inspiration from the iconic styles of J.C Leyendecker and Roger Wilkerson. This choice of aesthetic perfectly captures the essence of the WW2 era, allowing players to immerse themselves in its atmosphere.

Annihilation44 is being made in collaboration with the creators of Europa: The Last Battle to bring unparalleled historical depth as well as immersive story telling to bring an interactive experience like no other.

Etherin

New!

Etherin is an Operating System that not only controls every single thing in your home smart or not but it integrates into your life to make every day life easier unlike a smart home. Etherin will run on custom hardware made by us and will have support to run on a variety of different systems in the future. This may include support for retro x86 cpu's.

Vance Revival

Success!

We revived the Vance project back in September 2022 and after about a year of work we have successfully gotten the project back up on it's feet.

Vance is a reimagining of Half-Life 2 from the point of view of the deuteragonist Alyx Vance. In the universe of Vance, Gordon Freeman never returns and is presumed dead, leaving Alyx in his place.
